About the Center

Malam Yahaya Digital Technology Center, established by the Hajiya Fatima Yahaya Foundation, is a dedicated ICT facility focused on delivering reliable CBT services, hands‑on ICT training, and community development programs. Our mission is to empower individuals, schools and organizations with digital skills and solutions that improve education, employability and innovation.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower individuals, schools, and organizations by providing ICT knowledge and digital solutions that enhance education, improve employability, foster innovation, and promote sustainable community development.

Vision: To be a leading digital hub in Northern Nigeria recognized for excellence in CBT delivery, ICT training and community impact.

Programmes

Computer for Children

Foundational ICT programme for young learners. Covers: basic operations, MS Office, internet safety, and introductory programming.

Target: Primary and Junior Secondary School Students.

Delivery: Physical at Fatima Yahaya International School, Sifawa.

Certificate, Engineering & Diploma Programmes (UDUCONS)

Structured certificate and diploma courses in Computer Studies delivered in affiliation with Usmanu Danfodiyo University Consultancy Services (UDUCONS).

Target: Senior secondary leavers, teachers and professionals.

Note: These programmes are affiliated to Usmanu Danfodiyo University Consultancy Services (UDUCONS).

Professional ICT Courses

Specialized technical courses on Graphic Design, Database, Networking, Data Analysis in collaboration with Cisco Academy.

Bootcamps and Workshops

Days and Weeks Short intensive programmes in Cybersecurity, Web Development, Machine Learning, Blockchain and Artificial Intelligence designed to produce projects and prototypes for incubation.

CBT for School Terminal Exams

We offer schools the opportunity to run their terminal exams on our CBT platform. Schools provide questions and student lists; the Center uploads and administers exams under simulated JAMB/WAEC conditions. Reports and analytics are delivered to schools for review.

  • Benefits: student familiarity with CBT, standardized results, and reduced malpractice risk.
  • Capacity: Up to 300 candidates per session (batch scheduling available).
  • Fee Model: Affordable per-student pricing with school discount packages.
